## Agent Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Farmers Tab
View and manage farmers in your assigned regions:
- **Search:** Find farmers by name, phone, or district
- **Filter:** Filter by district or status (Active/Inactive)
- **View:** See farmer contact details and status
- **Note:** Read-only access - you cannot edit farmer information

### 2️⃣ Negotiations Tab
Monitor and facilitate price negotiations:
- **View:** See all negotiations between buyers and farmers in your region
- **Add Internal Notes:** Document your observations (private notes)
- **Mark as Connected:** Flag when you've connected the parties
- **Escalate to Admin:** Send problematic negotiations to admin with a reason

### 3️⃣ Alerts Tab
Stay updated with regional activities:
- **New Farmer Registrations:** Get notified when farmers join your region
- **Negotiation Updates:** Track status changes in negotiations
- **Region Assignments:** See when new regions are assigned to you
- **Unread Badge:** Shows count of unread notifications

## Agent Responsibilities

### ✅ What You Can Do:
- View farmers in your assigned regions
- Monitor negotiations in your region
- Add internal notes to negotiations
- Connect buyers and farmers
- Escalate issues to admin
- View regional alerts and notifications

### ❌ What You Cannot Do:
- Edit or delete farmer accounts
- Modify product prices
- Approve or reject negotiations
- Access data outside your assigned regions
- Change negotiation status (except marking as connected)

## Admin Features for Agent Management

### For Admins:
Admins can manage agents through the Admin Dashboard → Users Tab → Agents:

1. **Add New Agent:**
   - Click "Add New Agent" button
   - Fill in: Name, Email, Phone, Password
   - Select Primary District (from 25 Sri Lankan districts)
   - Assign Regions (can select multiple from 9 regions)
   - Set Status (Active/Inactive)
   - Click "Add Agent"

2. **Edit Agent Status:**
   - Click edit icon next to agent
   - Change status between Active/Inactive
   - Save changes

3. **Delete Agent:**
   - Click delete icon (trash)
   - Confirm deletion

## Common Tasks

### Task 1: Help a Buyer Connect with a Farmer
1. Go to **Negotiations Tab**
2. Find the negotiation
3. Add an internal note with your observations
4. Click "Mark as Connected" once you've facilitated contact
5. Monitor the negotiation progress

### Task 2: Escalate a Problematic Negotiation
1. Go to **Negotiations Tab**
2. Find the negotiation that needs admin intervention
3. Click "Escalate to Admin"
4. Provide a clear reason (e.g., "Price dispute beyond my authority")
5. Admin will be notified and take over

### Task 3: Check New Farmers in Your Region
1. Go to **Farmers Tab**
2. Use the search or filter to find specific farmers
3. Check their contact details and status
4. Reach out via phone if needed for support

### Task 4: Review Regional Alerts
1. Go to **Alerts Tab**
2. Review unread notifications (marked with blue badge)
3. Check for new farmer registrations
4. Monitor negotiation status updates

## Tips for Effective Agent Work

✨ **Best Practices:**
- Check alerts daily for new activity in your region
- Add clear, concise internal notes to negotiations
- Only escalate when truly necessary (price disputes, conflicts, etc.)
- Maintain regular contact with farmers in your region
- Keep your office contact information updated

⚠️ **Important Notes:**
- You can only see data from YOUR assigned regions
- Internal notes are visible to other agents and admins, not buyers/farmers
- Marking as "Connected" doesn't change negotiation status
- Escalated negotiations go directly to admin dashboard

## Getting Help

If you need assistance:
1. Contact your admin through the platform
2. Check your assigned regions in the Alerts tab → Contact Information
3. Use the escalation feature for urgent issues
